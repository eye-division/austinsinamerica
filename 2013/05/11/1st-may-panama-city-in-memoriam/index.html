Austins in Americas

Inspired by John Coleman's drive in 1959, five crew will drive three Austin 7's the 11,000 miles from Buenos Aires to New York City through twelve countries and two continents. Arrival in Times Square is planned for noon on May 31st 2013. Read the blogs below for progress and news.
